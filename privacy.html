Privacy Policy

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what information Supreme Auto ("we", "us") collects through this website, why we collect it, and how it's used. Supreme Auto is a vehicle dealership based at 546 Rachel de Beer St, Pretoria North, Pretoria.

Information We Collect

We collect information you choose to give us directly, through the following forms on this site:

  • The Contact / Enquiry form — name, phone number, email address, and the vehicle and message you provide.
  • The Finance Application Wizard — personal, employment and vehicle-finance details needed to submit a finance application on your behalf.
  • The WhatsApp Enquiry form on a vehicle's page — your name, phone number and any message you add.

Submitting the Contact form or a WhatsApp enquiry opens WhatsApp with a pre-filled message for you to review and send yourself — at that point your message is sent directly to our WhatsApp number, not stored by this website. The Finance Application Wizard prepares an email to our dealership email address for you to send via your own email client.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit only to respond to your enquiry, process a finance application, or otherwise assist with your vehicle purchase. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

This site embeds a Google Maps location and links to WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram and TikTok. When you interact with those features, the respective third party's own privacy policy applies to that interaction.

Cookies

This website does not use tracking or advertising cookies. Your browser may store small amounts of data locally (for example, to remember your progress through the Finance Application Wizard) — this stays on your device and is cleared automatically once your application is submitted or you choose to start over.

Your Rights

You may ask us at any time what information we hold about you, or ask us to correct or delete it, by contacting us using the details below.
